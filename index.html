Sagittarius

November 22 - December 21
Zodiac Symbol: Archer

Element: Fire
Sign Ruler: Jupiter
Quality: Mutable

Sagittarians are optimistic, lovers of freedom, hilarious, fair-minded, honest and intellectual. They are spontaneous and fun, usually with a lot of friends, and are perhaps the best conversationalists in the zodiac (maybe tied with Gemini).

Capricorn

December 21 - January 20
Zodiac Symbol: Sea Goat

Element: Earth
Sign Ruler: Saturn
Quality: Cardinal

Capricorns are overachievers, persistent, practical, and sensitive. They're known for their hardworking habits, and they're overachievers who make success look effortless — even though they put a lot of time and care into their careers and relationships.

Aquarius

January 20 - February 18
Zodiac Symbol: Water-Bearer

Element: Air
Sign Ruler: Uranus
Quality: Fixed

Aquarians are highly intellectual and creative, Walker says. Marked by independence, they don't like to be instructed what to do. While they can be social, they are not likely to participate in social interactions unless they truly want to.

Virgo

August 23 - September 23
Zodiac Symbol: The Maiden

Element: Earth
Sign Ruler: Mercury
Quality: Mutable

Virgo is a person born between August 23rd and September 22nd. Virgos are known for being practical, sensible, and loyal. They make excellent friends and partners. Virgos are known for being perfectionists, and can be meticulous and single-minded in their pursuit of improvement.

Libra

September 23 - October 23
Zodiac Symbol: Scales

Element: Air
Sign Ruler: Venus
Quality: Cardinal

Libras are known for being charming, beautiful, and well-balanced. They thrive on making things orderly and aesthetically pleasing. They also crave balance, and they can be equally as self-indulgent as they are generous. Libras are also the kings and queens of compromise, and they like making peace between others.

Aries

March 20 - April 20
Zodiac Symbol: Ram

Element: Fire
Sign Ruler: Mars
Quality: Cardinal

Like their fellow fire signs, Leo and Sagittarius, Aries is a passionate, motivated, and confident leader who builds community with their cheerful disposition and relentless determination. Uncomplicated and direct in their approach, they often get frustrated by exhaustive details and unnecessary nuances.

Scorpio

October 23 - November 22
Zodiac Symbol: Scorpion

Element: Water
Sign Ruler: Pluto
Quality: Fixed

As a fixed water sign, Scorpios are known for their loyalty and devotion — and also their passion. In fact, they are known to have such intense personalities that they are often confused with vibrant fire signs. Alas, they just have deep feelings and emotions.

Taurus

April 20 - May 21
Zodiac Symbol: Bull

Element: Earth
Sign Ruler: Venus
Quality: Fixed

Taurus traits include being hard-headed, down-to-earth, tenacious, reliable, loyal, and sensual. All of these characteristics mirror themes covered by the Second House of Self-Worth and Income, which Taurus rules.

Pisces

February 18 - March 20
Zodiac Symbol: Two Fish

Element: Water
Sign Ruler: Neptune
Quality: Mutable

Pisces people are known for being emotionally sensitive, gracious, and emotionally aware. Pisces characters are regarded for being among the most sympathetic of the zodiac signs, and they will go to great lengths to ensure the happiness of those around them. They're also creative and imaginative.

Gemini

May 21 - June 21
Zodiac Symbol: Twins

Element: Air
Sign Ruler: Mercury
Quality: Mutable

Playful and intellectually curious, Gemini is constantly juggling a variety of passions, hobbies, careers, and friend groups. They are the social butterflies of the zodiac: These quick-witted twins can talk to anyone about anything. Find them buzzing between happy hours, dinner parties, and dance floors.

Leo

July 22 - August 23
Zodiac Symbol: Lion

Element: Fire
Sign Ruler: Sun
Quality: Fixed

Leo traits include being confident, comfortable being the center of attention, drama-adoring, ambitious, loyal, fiercely protective of their nearest and dearest, generous, luxury-loving, sunny, and big-hearted.

Cancer

June 21 - July 22
Zodiac Symbol: Crab

Element: Water
Sign Ruler: Moon
Quality: Cardinal

Cancers have a reputation for being hyper emotional, temperamental, and spiteful. Cancers, in additional to being devoted, are extremely fond of their loved ones, often to an unhealthy degree. They place a high value on family and close friends, and will go to great lengths to defend them, no matter the price.
